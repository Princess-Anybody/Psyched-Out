Why is taking break important?

Breaks increase productivity and creativity. Working for long stretches without breaks leads to stress and exhaustion. Taking breaks refreshes the mind, replenishes your mental resources, and helps you become more creative. If you're studying, know that the human mind has an average attention span of 15 minutes. Take a short 5 minute break and continue. This will also help you remember information!
