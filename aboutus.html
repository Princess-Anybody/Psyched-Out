Mission Statement

The reason we created this website is to educate people with regards to mental health and psychology. Throughout this webpage, you will see statistics about mental health, hotlines and websites, and many other resources in which you can rely on. Our mission is to raise awarness about mental health, but also provide support to those in need. We believe everyone should be able to reach these resources and information regardless of race, ethnicity, religion, etc.
